# Moodboard

## Visual Direction
- Dark, moody, high-contrast pixel art.
- Vampire Survivors and Halls of Torment readability.
- Clear silhouettes and color-coded enemies.

## Targets
- Small sprite sizes for huge swarms.
- Big muzzle flashes and impact effects.
- Stronghold pieces read as modular tiles.

## UI
- Minimal HUD, focus on battlefield.
- Emphasis on build hotkeys and resource count.
